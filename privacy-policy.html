SudoPlay Privacy Policy Effective Date: June 8, 2026 1. Overview SudoPlay ("the App") is a Sudoku puzzle game developed by Ozgur Mazlum ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. 2. Information We Do Not Collect SudoPlay does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically: We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact details. We do not collect your location. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties. 3. Game Center SudoPlay uses Apple Game Center to provide leaderboard functionality. When you submit a score, Apple processes that information in accordance with Apple's own Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center credentials or personal Apple account information. Game Center leaderboards include: Best Time – Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert, and Extreme Total Puzzles Solved Participation in Game Center is optional and managed entirely through your device's Game Center settings. 4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device SudoPlay stores game progress, settings, and statistics locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults / Core Data). This data never leaves your device unless you use iOS system features such as iCloud Backup, which are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. 5. Children's Privacy SudoPlay is rated 4+ and is designed for all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the App collects no personal data at all, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. 6. Third-Party Services SudoPlay does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services beyond Apple Game Center. No data is shared with advertisers or data brokers. 7. Security Because SudoPlay does not transmit personal data, there is no network transmission of personal information to protect. All local data is protected by the security mechanisms provided by iOS. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised Effective Date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 9.
